Overview

Nepal is officially the Federal Democratic Republic of Nepal, a landlocked country in South Asia. It is located in the Himalayas and shares borders with China and India.

Geography

Nepal has a diverse geography, including fertile plains, subalpine hills, and the world's tallest mountains like Mount Everest.

History

The name "Nepal" has historical roots from the Vedic period. Ancient Nepal saw the rise of Hinduism and later became an important region for Buddhism, where Gautama Buddha was born in Lumbini.

Culture

Nepal is multi-ethnic, multi-lingual, and multi-religious with Nepali as its official language.
